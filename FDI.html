FDI for FY 2020-21

Visualising RBI's Foreign Investment data

  • Tools: Excel & Tableau
  • Data Source: Rerserve Bank of India
  • Project date: 18 June, 2021
  • Project URL: Dashboard Link

description:

Analysis of the foreign direct investments in the fiscal year 2020-21, examining the trend for the respective year, the analysis highlights the top 10 investor countries for past three years and the states with highest inward investments. The study also shows the trend for the past 20 years followed by the sectors that attract high investments. Lastly, forcast for next three years.
