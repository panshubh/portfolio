Analysis of online sales

The company needs to study its growth by analyzing last three years data. Essentially, identifing top revenue sources, products, sales trend, and profit Vs revenue. The raw data provided was cleaned and structured in Excel before loading it on jupyter notbook for analysis.

The Data

To create the necessary data model for doing analysis and fulfilling the business needs defined, the raw data provided in excel format was cleaned and arranged.

Customer Segmentation

Based on individual customer(user id) purchases, each customer's RFM score was calculated on the scale of 0-444(0-lowest,444-highest).

This gives a clear picture of the companies loyal customers and nearly lost customers. On these results the marketing team could employ special strategies for each group.

The above chart shows us the clusters of customers and the relation between recency and value of the product/service bought.

The green cluster is clearly the company's most valuable set of customer base, as they are frequent and spend more money.

Top selling plans

The treemap highlights the company's top 10 most sold regimen plans.

These insights can be sliced further by age and time of the year to understand the customer buying pattern and help the marketing team to target the campaigns precisely.

Revenue Vs Profit

The yearly profit against the revenue can be found with the area chart.

The proft made can be seen from the 4th quarter of year 2018, since then the profit growth is seen to be positive.
